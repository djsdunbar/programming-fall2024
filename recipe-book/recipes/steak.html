Spencer's Steak Recipe

Description

This is a delicious recipe to have for a family dinner, a romantic date or just because you want. This recipe is easy and quick so you don't have to worry about waiting for a long time. Follow the steps below to get the best medium well done steak!

Ingredients

  • Sirloin Steak
  • Butter
  • Thyme
  • Seasoning: Salt, Pepper, Garlic Power, Extra Steak seasoning
  • Oil
  • garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Make sure the steak is room temepeture and coat both side of the steak in oil in a bowl.
  2. Add your Seasonings on the steak and make sure the steak is coated in seasoning.
  3. Get a cast iron skillet, add some butter(or oil) and make sure the skillet gets hot.
  4. Once the skillet is add a good temperture put the steak in the skillet, and let your first side cook for 4 minutes.
  5. Once the first side is done flip it over and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. During the last minutes add thyme and/ or garlic and baste the steak for the last minute.
  7. Let the steak rest for 4-5 minutes.
  8. Cut up the steak and Enjoy!
